How the Golden Formula App Works

The app is split into a few focused pieces that all work together:

  • Golden Simulator: You upload a chart screenshot or paste numbers, and the engine reads price, highs/lows, and moving averages. It then runs my rules to decide if there’s a clean setup, what direction I’d lean, and where I’d place entry, stop, and target.
  • Options Picker: Once a setup is found, the options page builds an options chain around your entry, highlights the contract I’d choose, and explains why—timeframe, moneyness, risk, and how it fits the setup.
  • Golden Rules: A page that lays out the core rules I trade by, so you can see the logic behind the engine.
  • Golden Discipline: A page focused on discipline, emotional control, and the mental side of trading—because the best logic in the world doesn’t matter if you can’t control your impulses.
  • Glossary: A full glossary so any word you don’t understand, you can look up instantly while you’re using the app.
