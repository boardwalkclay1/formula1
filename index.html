How to Use the Golden Formula Simulator

The simulator is designed so you don’t have to guess what to type. You can simply upload a screenshot of your chart, and the app will read the numbers for you using OCR (image-to-text). From there, it runs everything through my personal rule engine and gives you guidance as if I’m right there with you.

Here’s the flow:

  • 1. Upload a chart screenshot: Take a clear screenshot of your stock chart (with price, highs/lows, and moving averages visible) and upload it below.
  • 2. Auto-read the numbers: The app uses Tesseract to read the text from the image and fill in the price, day high/low, and moving averages.
  • 3. My logic runs on your chart: Once the numbers are in, the Golden Formula engine checks for flags, double tops/bottoms, rounding patterns, even-number reactions, and moving average behavior—exactly how I would.
  • 4. You get a clear, human-style explanation: The output tells you the direction, entry, stop, target, and why the setup matters, in plain language.

This is built to help you overcome the isolation of trading alone. Instead of staring at a chart wondering what to do, you can let the simulator walk you through the logic I use every day. It won’t make you skip the work—but it will make the work feel guided.
