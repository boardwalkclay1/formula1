How the Golden Formula App Works

The app is built to guide you step‑by‑step through the same process I use every day:

  • Golden Simulator: Upload a chart screenshot and the app reads the price, highs/lows, and moving averages. It then runs my personal rule engine to determine the direction, entry, stop, target, and strength of the setup.
  • Golden Chart: The simulator draws a clean chart with your entry, stop, and target marked — so you can visualize the trade exactly how I would.
  • Options Picker: Once a setup is found, the options page builds a full options chain around your entry, highlights the contract I would choose, and explains why — timeframe, moneyness, risk, and how it fits the setup.
  • Golden Rules: A breakdown of the core rules I trade by. These are the same rules the engine uses.
  • Golden Discipline: A page dedicated to emotional control, discipline, and the mental side of trading — because the best logic in the world means nothing if you can’t control yourself.
  • Glossary: A full glossary so any word you don’t understand, you can look up instantly.
